Universe & Thesis
9,174 of 33,687 Targetable Locations Pass the Top-Quartile Screen. The Best Markets Sit Between Scarcity and Saturation.
This report covers the U.S. veterinary services market as a geographic acquisition-screening analysis. The core thesis is not a density story. The most useful acquisition markets are CBSAs where high-quality target depth and sourcing breadth overlap, without the local competitive pressure that suppresses pricing and erodes defensibility. This report identifies where to focus, what to diligence, and which market types require caution. It is a market screen, not a target-level underwriting model.

Five Findings
The Midwest is the most balanced region. Highest top-quartile share at 31.3% with the lowest platform-owned context density.
The South has scale but requires filtering. Largest target pool at 10,954 locations; 61.4% high-pressure share demands CBSA-level prioritization.
Z3 is the peak acquisition zone. Average STOC Geo Index 73.1, top-quartile share 68.3%. The best opportunities are where supply is proven but not immediately crowded.
High-pressure markets almost never pass top-quartile. 20,629 high-pressure targets produce just 32 top-quartile screens (0.2% share).
De novo is a separate, narrower screen. 507 locations (166 strong + 341 possible) pass the de novo screen. It uses different inputs (supply gap, population coverage) and identifies different market types. Acquisition and de novo lists should always be kept and used separately.

8,903 Locations Pass the Acquisition-Led Screen. 507 Pass the De Novo Screen. These Are Different Markets With Different Logic. Do Not Merge Them.
Acquisition-led markets have strong STOC Geo Index scores but often negative supply gaps, meaning supply already serves local demand. De novo markets are identified by positive or near-zero supply gaps, low existing density, and low competitive pressure. A market that scores well for acquisition may actively screen poorly for new-location expansion, and vice versa.

External Report Use Controls
CBSA rankings use 762 validated labels
All CBSA-level rankings in this report are based on the 762 validated CBSA labels. 27,776 target rows have validated CBSA designations; 5,911 remain in the universe but are excluded from CBSA rankings.
Platform-owned locations are anonymized competitive context

Population-derived metrics apply to 29,057 rows
